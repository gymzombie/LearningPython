
Table 1-1

Operator    Operation     Example     Evaluates to...
**          Exponent        2**3          8
%           Modulus/Remainder 22%8        6
//          Integer division/floored quotient   22//8   2
/           Division          22/8        2.75
*           Multiplication    3*5         15
-           Subtraction       5-2         3
+           Addition          2+2         4


Order of operations is similar to math. ** is first, *, /, //, and % next, + and - are last, from left to right. 
Parenthesis can override precidence if needed.
